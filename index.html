My Services

Drowsiness Detection

A front cam is fixed facing the driver which detects whether the eyes of the driver is closed or not. The driver’s face and eyes are detected separately by the drowsiness model.

Accident Detection

The rear cam which detects lane also detect accidents occurred at the front. After detection it alerts the driver about the accident to prevent further chaos.

Steering Button Sensor

Input from the steering’s sensor is used to detect a driver’s consciousness while the eyes are wide open.
